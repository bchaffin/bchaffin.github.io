### The Topswops game

[Topswops](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Topswops) is a game invented by John Conway. The procedure is simple: start with a deck of _N_ cards, numbered 1 through _N_, shuffled. Look at the number of the card on top, then reverse the order of that many cards on the top of the deck. Continue until the top card is a 1.
